Our Story

Born from a bad experience.
Built for your protection.

Dynamyte Technologies is a Zimbabwe-based mobile device retail business founded on a single, powerful principle: consumers deserve to know exactly what they are buying.

In a market flooded with refurbished phones sold deceptively as brand new, Dynamyte Technologies stands apart as the only retailer committed to radical transparency, honest grading, and genuine after-sale support.

"I personally experienced the damage of buying a misrepresented Samsung S21 Ultra — its LCD destroyed by a firmware update — and an iPhone with a degraded, non-original battery. Nobody warned me. Nobody disclosed anything. I knew then that there was a clear and urgent gap in our market." — Founder, Dynamyte Technologies

That experience didn't just inspire a business — it defined its non-negotiable rules. No deception. No grey-area selling. Every product sold at Dynamyte carries a full grading guarantee — Grade A, B, or C — with complete disclosure of condition, battery health, and parts originality before you part with a single dollar.

Our immediate focus is iPhones, with a planned expansion into Android devices, laptops, tablets, and accessories — all held to the same uncompromising standard of transparency that defines everything we do.

The Dynamyte Standard

Our Grading Guarantee

Every device is graded before listing. You know exactly what you're buying — condition, battery health, and parts originality — before you pay a cent.

A

Grade A

Excellent condition
  • Minimal to no cosmetic wear — looks near new
  • Battery health 88% or above
  • All original Apple parts — screen, battery, cameras
  • Face ID / Touch ID fully functional
  • No iCloud lock, fully erased and ready
Best value · Premium pick
B

Grade B

Good condition
  • Light scratches or scuffs visible on close inspection
  • Battery health 78%–87%
  • All core functions fully working
  • May have one non-original part (disclosed upfront)
  • No iCloud lock, ready to use
Honest · Affordable · Disclosed
C

Grade C

Fair condition
  • Visible wear — scratches, minor dents noted
  • Battery health 65%–77% (replacement advised)
  • All features functional despite cosmetic wear
  • Parts originality fully disclosed in listing
  • No iCloud lock — lowest price tier
Budget pick · No hidden surprises
